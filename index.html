Welcome to Sac State's Data Science Club


Our mission is to bring together Sac State students who are interested in data science to learn more about different technologies and tools, and make some really cool projects.


We do this by creating an inclusive environment that independent learning, collaboration, and camaraderie.

Projects


Here are some projects we have made on project days/as hackathon teams:

^^^ Hover!

March Madness Predictor

Visualization tool to rank teams by their offensive and defensive ability. Built in ~14 hours for CSUS ECS Open House.

Clean Sac

311 Data collection visualization tool. Can view incident heatmaps by type filters and by cluster location. Built at Hack4Sac.

Couch Coder

Couch Coder is a IDE for google docs that supports syntax highlighting! Built at HackDavis, won best dev tool.

Iidata Timed Contest

A 3 hour timed data analysis contest at UC Davis Statistics Club's data science conference where we won first place.

HackDavis 2017

A way to track UC Davis' energy usage, winner of OSI soft's first place prize at HackDavis 2017. Built by Matthew Merrill, David Judilla, and Brandon Sherman.
